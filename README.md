## 🚀 Project Overview
This repository presents a **professional, enterprise-level overview of Supply Chain Management (SCM)** covering **process flows, operational stages, KPIs, analytics use cases, and industry best practices**.

It is built for:
- Supply Chain & Operations professionals  
- Data Analysts & BI Developers  
- Control Tower & Planning teams  
- Interview & portfolio preparation  

The framework follows **real-world enterprise SCM standards** used across manufacturing, e-commerce, and logistics organizations.

---

## 🧭 What is Supply Chain Management?
**Supply Chain Management (SCM)** is the coordinated management of **materials, information, and financial flows** across the entire product lifecycle — from **demand planning** to **customer delivery and returns**.

> 🎯 Goal: Deliver the right product, in the right quantity, at the right place, at the right time, at the lowest total cost.
